FAQ's

About Mira Developments

Who is Mira Developments?

Mira Developments is a forward-thinking real estate company headquartered in Dubai, with a growing portfolio in Ras Al Khaimah. Their vision focuses on crafting exclusive living experiences in partnership with world-renowned luxury brands.

What sets Mira Developments apart from other developers?

Mira collaborates with elite design houses like Trussardi, Elie Saab, Bentley, and Gianfranco Ferré—bringing Italian craftsmanship and fashion-driven aesthetics to their residential projects.

Where are Mira's projects located?

Mira currently has landmark developments in Al Furjan (Dubai), Meydan (Dubai), and Al Marjan Island (Ras Al Khaimah), with future expansions in other emerging luxury communities.

Are Mira projects suitable for investment?

Yes. With branded interiors, strong ROI potential, and locations near major lifestyle and tourism hubs, Mira's developments are ideal for end-users and investors alike.

What payment plans are available?

Mira offers flexible post-handover payment plans, typically structured with 10–20% booking, 40–50% during construction, and the remainder on or after handover.
